Axolotl Pack
This add-on was dared by friend and inspired by the meme that PhoenixSC made.
Features:
- Axolotls
- Axolotls now drop raw axolotl when killed.
- When killed by fire aspect it will drop cooked axolotls.
- Drops are affected by looting
- Raw Axolotl
- Can smelt items up to 2 minutes
- When eaten, will give regeneration 3 for 10 secs. Fills 1 hunger point
- Cooked Axolotl
- Can smelt 1 item
- When eaten, will drain 10XP levels from you. Fills 10 hunger point
